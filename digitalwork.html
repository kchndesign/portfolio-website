Digital Work

Free Spirits Dance Website

freespiritsdance.com

Free Spirits Dance Co. is a small business in Haymarket, Sydney. We began developing a new website during the 2021 lockdown with a goal to reinvent the business by the time we opened up.

This involved many meetings with the stakeholders, talking through their vision for their business post-covid and devising ways for their digital presence to support that.

My role as their designer was to make sure that there is a consistent and recognisable brand identity, as well as an effective digital presence that is laser focused on their business goals.
