About Me

I am a graduating product designer who is motivated by using design and technology to help real people and businesses. I love everything techical, from CAD and product rendering to programming microcontrollers and building websites. I am excited to be able to combine those skills with a deep understanding of product research to create empathy-driven designs that directly address a user’s concerns. I particularly love designs that are able to employ feelings of joy and whimsy to connect to people. I have experience using my skills to help businesses develop their digital presence.

Delta Drill

This ‘Rowenta’ Drill concept was designed as a response to a brief that required me to imagine if Rowenta sold a power drill. It was an exercise that was both aesthetically and technically challenging, as we were required to design around a predetermined electro-mechanical package.

Micro Hose Nozzle

This is a user-centred product concept that is designed with the emerging gardener in mind. For those who are getting into gardening but might not have the most space to use garden hoses, this concept combines spray bottle action with a hose fitting for multi-mode functionality.
